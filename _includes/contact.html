About me

My name is Karolina Soltys and I'm a software engineer moonlighting as a game designer. I am dreaming of someday making game design my full-time job. Larps are my passion verging on obsession, and with Arsenic & Lies I am hoping to introduce larping to people who haven't tried it before. I co-organise The Immersivist Club, a group regularly playing larps in London, and The Smoke – a larp festival taking place in London every January. I've also designed and organised a couple of weekend larps: Our Last Year and The Castle, and co-organised the 2019 UK run of Just A Little Lovin'.

Acknowledgements

Arsenic & Lies is inspired by some great games: Unheroes by Joanna Piancastelli and Fairweather Manor by Dziobak Studios – I do recommend you check these out, they are a great introduction to larps. And, of course, my teenage love for Agatha Christie novels, and more recent binging on Downton Abbey.

The artwork on cards is public domain; the cover artwork was created by myself with touch-ups by Oliver Nøglebæk. The larp is in no way endorsed by Downton Abbey or the Agatha Christie estate and does not use any content from these works – they are just inspiration.
