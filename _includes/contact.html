About me

My name is a Karolina Soltys and I'm a software engineer moonlighting as a game designer. I am dreaming of someday making game design my full-time job. Larps are my passion verging on obsession, and with Arsenic & Lies I am hoping to introduce larping to people who haven't tried it before. I co-organise The Immersivist Club, a group regularly playing larps in London, and The Smoke – a larp festival taking place in London every January. I also write some more artsy larps, which you can check out on my personal site.

Arsenic & Lies is inspired by some great games: Unheroes by Joanna Piancastelli, Will That Be All? and A Taste for Murder by Graham Walmsley, and Fairweather Manor by Dziobak Studios – I do recommend you check these out, they are a great introduction to larps. And, of course, my teenage love for Agatha Christie novels, and more recent binging on Downton Abbey.

The artwork is either public domain or created by myself. The larp is in no way endorsed by Downton Abbey or the Agatha Christie estate and does not use any content from these works – they are just inspiration.
